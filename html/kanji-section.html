    • Entering これは漢字を含む文章だ gives the kanji 漢, 字, 含, 文, 章
  • Search kanji by details using the middle and right entry, e.g.:
    • Kanji which have a meaning "search": search
    • Kanji which have a kun-yomi "さがす": さがす
    • Kanji which have an on-yomi "ソウ": ソウ
    • Use a wildcard to match an arbitrary number of characters, e.g.:
      Kanji with a kun-yomi starting with "さが": さが*
    • Multiple details can be separated by space/comma/semicolon, e.g.:
      Kanji with both a kun-yomi さがす and on-yomi ソウ: さがす, ソウ
